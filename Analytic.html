Overview

New York City consists of five boroughs. In each region there are many different neighborhoods. Many sales occur for very small dollar amounts: most often $0. This sale is actually a deed transfer between parties, for example, a parent transfers ownership of their home to a child after moving to retire and perhaps several other factors.
